Overview

Bryllance is aiming to help freelancers offering personalised services source for freelance jobs opportunities and grow their business. Bryllance motivated to help the freelance industry grow, decrease reliance on third party agencies and return power to the individual freelancer and consumer. Bryllance selected Code Symphony as a technology partner to convert their idea into a reality - a viable product to help them flourish in the market.
