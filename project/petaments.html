Petaments (An E-Commerce Website)

Duration

January 2021 - Present

Client

Petaments

Location

Europe

Platform

Web

Technologies

NextJS, NodeJS, ParseServer, PostgreSQL, React material UI, Stripe integration, Paypal integration

Overview

Together with vets, nutritionists and dog owners Petaments have spent a lot of time to better understand which health issues are most common in dogs and how overall life quality can be improved with the right nutrition. They wanted to build a platform where they can offer products based on the changing needs of the dogs. Petaments wants to make sure that every dogs gets all the nutrients he/she needs to lead a long, happy, and healthy life.

The Situation

PETAMENTS' ambitious aim to provide nutritional products to every pets

Petaments wanted a creatively crafted website mainly for pet lovers. They wanted a platform which provides all in one solutions for pet lovers to address various concern regarding the health of their loving pets. Based on the analytical examination of various vital factors, customers are suggested the list of healthy food products. The customers is briefed with every detail possible about the product and Petaments motive to look after the well-being of their pets.

Petaments engaged Code Symphony to co-develop this complex software solution. From the very first weeks of working together, Petaments felt that the team at Code Symphony understood their challenges and knew how to deliver the platform.

The Solution

A creatively crafted platform for pet lovers to address various concerns regarding the health of their loving pets.

Led by their clear vision, we helped PETAMENTS to develop creatively crafted website for pet lovers to address various concerns regarding the health of their loving pets.

The first task was to develop the basic architecture, with two main generalized foundation Front end and Back end. After mapping out the blue print the front end was composed of Admin and webapp. Webapp was modelled with integration of modern payment methods, namely PayPal and Stripe. The front end was equipped with custom algorithm used to generate suggestions for different product based on the visitor’s input.
