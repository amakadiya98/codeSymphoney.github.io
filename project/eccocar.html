Reservation Management System (RMS)

Eccocar wanted a platform where their partners can launch their own shared mobility services within short period of time. Eccocar wanted a Digital solution that can be integrated between Eccocar and their partner's RMS, where partners can choose the modules they want to operate with. Below are the features that users would be able to use within the RMS to improve their car rental strategy and operational efficiency.

Manage Users and User groups
Manage different Vehicles
Manage Reservations
Manage Billing for the reservation
Manage hierarchy within the organization
Detailed reports on fleet usage and mobility costs
Set Alerts on important events

White Label App

We have built a car rental application where users can register, validate their documents, sign the contract and book services, inspect and unlock the vehicle from an app. We have also built a customizable interface where Eccocar can change language, logos, colors, and much more according to their partner needs.

IN THE END...

Eccocar managed to optimize their partner's operating costs by reducing rental management time by 65%. Eccocar also helped their partners in increasing the retention rate by 20%. We started developing ECCOCAR in Fall of 2018, and we are now launching Phase 2 of the product. Our partnership has spanned 650 hours, and will continue as we build new features. We expect ECCOCAR to soon transform the car rental industry by making the app more accessible and user-friendly to both the car rentals and customers globally.
